Situation Map of Greece:
Camps, Capacity and Red Cross Distributions

The Greek government has opened Emergency Reception Centres across the country. The Hellenic Red Cross, with support from the IFRC and multiple National Societies, is actively responding.

This map visualises the open camps across Greece alongside the supporting branches of the Hellenic Red Cross. Hover over any point to obtain more information. Locations with colored proportional circles represent the sites where the Red Cross is actively distributing items. Click these locations to learn more.

Please be aware: this map does not always display well with Internet Explorer browsers. For best results use Chrome or Firefox.
